Important Usage Rules

• Pending / Processing: Your order has been received and queued. It will start within the estimated start time mentioned in the service description.

• In Progress: Delivery has started, and the ordered quantity is being delivered gradually.

• Completed: The full quantity of your order has been delivered successfully.

• Partial: Only part of the order was delivered. The remaining amount is automatically refunded to your account balance.

• Canceled: The order could not be completed and has been fully refunded to your balance.

Do Not

• Do not place the same order on the same link or account from another panel while an order is already in progress on that link.

• Do not place a new order on the same link before the previous order is completed or refilled.

• Changing the username or link after placing an order may cause the order to be marked as completed. In such cases, the order will not be eligible for refund or refill.
